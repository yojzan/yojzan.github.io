The cleanest hands

Who is the thief in a house with no owner?
Who commits the sin
that the saint was too holy to notice?

I found you on a shelf
between the books and the marked-down candles,
your name a number,
your silence, for sale.

They poured the white on stone.
Outside, a mouth forgot what fullness was.
The stone drank well that morning.

I did not come here for you
and perhaps that is the only way
to come here clean.

What does it mean to steal
what was never meant to be kept?
What does it mean to keep
what you do not believe in?

I walked out.
You were under my coat.
weightless, the way only true things are,
or the way only empty things are.
