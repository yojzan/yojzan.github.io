ARGA

Second brain. Archive. Home of thought.

ARGA is a place where nothing is lost. Every thought that appears, stays here. It exists as a home for thinking, remembering, and returning.

It is not a system of output. It is a system of accumulation. A record of everything that was once alive in thought.

A visitor does not need direction here. You may begin anywhere, or nowhere. The only requirement is freedom from the need to “learn correctly”. This is not instruction. It is entry.
