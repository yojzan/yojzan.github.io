ARGA

Second brain. Archive. Home of thought.

ARGA is a place where nothing is lost.
Every thought that appears, stays here.
It exists as a home for thinking, remembering, and returning.

It is not a system of output. It is a system of accumulation.
A record of everything that was once alive in thought.
